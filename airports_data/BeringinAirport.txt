Beringin Airport (IATA: MTW, ICAO: WAOM) (Indonesian: Bandar Udara Beringin) is an airport in Muara Teweh, the capital city of the North Barito Regency in Central Kalimantan, Indonesia. Flight Destinations Currently there are 2 airlines operate schedule commercial flights from this Airport with Palangkaraya is popular route. Airlines and destinations connecting to Beringin Airport: Airline Name Destination Aviastar Palangkaraya Susi Air Banjarmasin, Palangkaraya Public Transportation Car and taxi: Various taxi services are provided by numerous service providers outside the arrival hall. Terminal Facilities The airport resides at an elevation of 127 ft (38.6 m) above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 13/31 with an asphalt surface measuring 904 m (2,966 ft).
