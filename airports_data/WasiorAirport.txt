Wasior Airport (Indonesia: Bandaran Wasior) (IATA: WSR, ICAO: WAUW) is an airport in Wasior, West Papua, Indonesia. The airport resides at an elevation of 7 m (23 ft) above mean sea level. It has one runway designated at 17/35 with an asphalt surface measuring 959 m (2.905 ft) in length. Susi Air are offers scheduled commercial flights from this Airport to Manokwari. Address: Jl.
