Kao Airport (Indonesian: Bandar Udara Kao) (IATA: KAZ, ICAO: WAEK) is the main airport serving Kao, a small coastal town on the eastern Indonesian island of Halmahera. It is located in the North Halmahera Regency, part of the province of North Maluku. There are regular scheduled flights by Lion Air (by Mentari) to Sam Ratulangi from this airport. The airport has one runway with an asphalt surface measuring 903 m (2,963 ft) in length. The Airport is operated and managed by UPT Ditjen Hubud. Address: Jl. Bandara , Kel. Jati Kec.Kao Kab.
