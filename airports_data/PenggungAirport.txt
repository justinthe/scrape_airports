Penggung Airport or Cakrabuwana Airport (IATA: CBN, ICAO: WICD) is a small airport located on Penggung, Cirebon City, West Java, Indonesia. The airport resides at an elevation of 27 m (89 ft) above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 04/22 with an asphalt surface measuring 1,259 m (4,131 ft) in length. The Airport is control and managed by Cirebon City Government. The Airport is hub for Susi Air and Trigana Air Service.
