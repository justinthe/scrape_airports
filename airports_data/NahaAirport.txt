Naha Airport (IATA: NAH, ICAO: WAMH) is an airport serving the locality of Tahuna, in the Sangir Islands, part of the North Sulawesi province of Indonesia. The airport resides at an elevation of 5 m (16 ft) above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 15/33 with an asphalt surface measuring 1,097 m (3,599 ft). There is weekly commercial flights by Trigana Air Service to Manado from this Airport. Address: Naha I, North Tabukan Kepulauan Sangihe Regency North Sulawesi Indonesia
