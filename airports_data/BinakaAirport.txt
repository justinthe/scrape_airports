Binaka Airport (IATA: GNS, ICAO: WIMB) is an airport in Gunung Sitoli, Nias Island, Indonesia. It is located in Jalan Pelabuhan Udara Binaka km. 19.5 Gunungsitoli. The Airport is located 21 km from the Gunung Sitoli city center. The Airport resides at an elevation of 6 m (20 ft) above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 09/27 with an asphalt surface measuring 1,335 m (4,380 ft) in length. The Airport is operated and managed by Indonesian Transportation Ministry.
