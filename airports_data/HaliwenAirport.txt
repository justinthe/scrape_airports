Haliwen Airport (IATA: ABU, ICAO: WATA, formerly WRKA) is an airport in Atambua, Indonesia. The airport resides at an elevation of 437.18 m (1,434 ft) above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 08/26 with an asphalt surface measuring 1,500 m (4,921 ft) in length. There are commercial schedule flights from Wings Air and Susi Air to Kupang and Alor from this Airport. Address: Jalan Marsada Adi Sucipto Belu Nusa Tenggara Timur 85711 Indonesia Tel: +62 389 21345
