Buli Airport (IATA: WUB, ICAO: WAEM) is an airport in Buli, near Maba the capital city of East Halmahera Regency, North Maluku. Indonesia. There are regular scheduled flights by Wings Air to Ternate from this airport. The airport resides at an elevation of 2 m (7 ft) above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 03/21 with an asphalt surface measuring 1,823 m (6,024 ft) in length. The Airport is operated and managed by East Halmahera Government. Address: Pekaulang, Maba East Halmahera Regency North Maluku Indonesia
