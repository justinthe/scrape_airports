Jaos Orno Imsula Airport (Indonesia: Bandar Udara Jos Orno Imsula) (IATA: JIO) is an airport serving the city of Tiakur, on the Moa island in the Southwest Maluku Regency of the Maluku province, Indonesia. The airport resides at an elevation of 4 m (13 ft) above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 12/30 with an asphalt surface measuring 1,000 m (3,281 ft) in length. The Airport is operated and managed by UPT Ditjen Hubud.
