Sultan Muhammad Kaharuddin III Airport (Indonesian: Bandar Udara Sultan Muhammad Kaharuddin III) (IATA: SWQ, ICAO: WADS), is an airport located in Sumbawa Besar, West Nusa Tenggara, Indonesia. It is officially named after the last reigned Sultan Muhammad Kaharuddin III of Sultanate of Sumbawa. Garuda Indonesia (by Explore) and Wings Air offer schedule commercial flights using ATR72-600 to Mataram from this Airport. The Airport is managed and controlled by UPT Ditjen Hubud, an agency under Ministry of Transportation, Indonesia. The airport resides at an elevation of 5.8 m (19 ft) above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 14/32 with an asphalt surface measuring 1,650 m (5,413 ft) in length. Address: Jl. Garuda No. 41 Sumbawa Besar Lempeh, Kec.
