Juvai Semaring Airport (Indonesian: Bandar Udara Juvai Semaring) (IATA: LBW, ICAO: WAQJ previously WRLB) is an airport serving Long Bawan, located in the province of North Kalimantan in Indonesia. It is also known as Long Bawan Airport. There are few small buildings there to used as passengers waiting and check-in terminal.
