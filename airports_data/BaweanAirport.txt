Bawean Airport (IATA: BXW), also known as Harun Thohir Airport, is an airport serving Bawean, an island in East Java Province, Indonesia. This airport began operations in January 2016. This airport expected to serve other cities in Indonesia to the Bawean Island. The airport resides at an elevation of 21 m (69 ft) above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 09/27 with an asphalt surface measuring 930 m (3,051 ft) in length. Airfast Indonesia offers schedule commercial flight to Surabaya from this Airport.
