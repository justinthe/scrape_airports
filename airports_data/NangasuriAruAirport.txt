Nangasuri Aru Airport (IATA: BJK, ICAO: WAPK) is an airport in Benjina, Maluku Province, Indonesia. Benjina is located in on the west side of the Aru Islands, south of the city of Dobo, on the island of Maikoor. There are no regular scheduled flights to this airport, but chartered or private planes can land at this air strip. The airport resides at an elevation of 25 m (82 ft) above mean sea level. It has one runway with an asphalt surface measuring 2,000 m (6,562 ft) in length. The Airport is operated and managed by PT Djanti Group. Address: Benjina, Aru Islands Maluku Regency Indonesia
