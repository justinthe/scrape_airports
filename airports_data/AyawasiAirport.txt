Ayawasi Airport (IATA: AYW, ICAO: WASA) is an airport in Ayawasi, Indonesia. It is being constructed to replace the Domine Edward Osok Airport in Sorong, but also serve the area around Sorong. The airport resides at an elevation of 549 m (1,800 ft) above mean sea level. It has one runway measuring 2020 m (6.600 ft) in length. Currently there are no commercial flights operate from this Airport. Address: Anggi Pegunungan Arfak Papua Barat Indonesia
