Cijulang Nusawiru Airport (Indonesian: Bandar Udara Nusawiru )(IATA: CJN, ICAO: WI1A), also known as Pangandaran Airport, is an airport near Pangandaran, Pangandaran Regency, a city in the province of West Java on the island of Java in Indonesia. The Airport is operated and managed by by UPT Ditjen Hubud, an agency under Ministry of Transportation, Indonesia. Susi Air offers schedule commercial flights to Jakarta (Halim Perdanakusuma) from this Airport. The airport resides at an elevation of 5 m (16 ft) above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 07/25 with an asphalt surface measuring 1,400 m (4,593 ft) in length. Address: Cijulang Pangandaran Regency West Java Indonesia
