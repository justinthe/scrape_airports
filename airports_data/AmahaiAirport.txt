Amahai Airport (IATA: AHI, ICAO: WAPA) is an airport in Amahai, Indonesia. Amahai is located on the south coast of the island of Seram, near the city of Masohi, and on the east side of the Elpaputih Bay. The airport resides at an elevation of 9 m (30 ft) above mean sea level. It has one runway with an asphalt surface measuring 2,713 m (8,901 ft) in length. Address: Soahuku, Amahai Central Maluku Regency Maluku Indonesia
