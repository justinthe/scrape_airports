Oksibil Airport (IATA: OKL, ICAO: WAJO) has connecting flights to Jayapura with Trigana Air Service and Wings Air. The airport is located about 1 km South West of the city center. The runway can handle Turboprop STOL aircraft but is able to handle larger Turboprop Regional airliner. The airport resides at an elevation of 1,307 m (4,288 ft) above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 11/29 with an asphalt surface measuring 885 m (2,904 ft) in length. There are few buildings that acts as a terminal structure as well as a small tower in the tarmac area beside the runway. Address: Kotdol, Serambakon Pegunungan Bintang Regency Papua 99573 Indonesia
