Haji Aroeppala Airport (IATA: KSR, ICAO: WAWH), sometimes abbreviated H. Aroeppala Airport, is an airport in Selayar Regency, South Sulawesi, Sulawesi Island, Indonesia.With the inauguration of the well equipped and modern terminal, the Airport now able to provided better and comfortable environment for its passengers. The operation of the Airport is managed by UPT Ditjen Hubud, an agency under Ministry of Transportation, Indonesia. Flight Destinations There are commercial schedule flights from H. Aroeppala Airport to destinations as below: Airline Name Destination Aviastar Bone, Kendari, Labuhan Bajo, Ruteng Susi Air Bima, Bone, Kendari Wings Air Makassar Technical Specification The airport resides at an elevation of 4 metres (13 ft) above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 16/34 with an asphalt surface measuring 1,950 m (6,398 ft). Address: Jl. Poros Padang Kel. Bontosunggu Kec. Bontoharu Kab.
