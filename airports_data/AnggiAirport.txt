Anggi Airport (IATA: AGD, ICAO: WASG) is an airport in Anggi, Papua, Indonesia. The airport resides at an elevation of 2,150 m (7,054 ft) above mean sea level. It has one runway measuring 425 m (1,394 ft) in length. Currently there are no commercial flights operate from this Airport. Address: Anggi Pegunungan Arfak Papua Barat Indonesia
