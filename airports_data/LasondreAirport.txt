Lasondre Airport (ICAO: WIMO, IATA: LSE) is a small airport located in Batu Islands (Indonesian: Pulau-Pulau Batu), South Nias, North Sumatra, Indonesia.. It has one runway designated 06L/24R with an asphalt surface measuring 1,400 m (4,593 ft) in length. Susi Air offers schedule commercial flights to Gunung Sitoli, Sibolga and Padang from this Airport.
