Kasiguncu Airport (Indonesian: Bandara Kasiguncu) (IATA: PSJ, ICAO: WAMP) is an airport in Poso, Central Sulawesi, Sulawesi Island, Indonesia. The Airport is located 15 km west from the Poso city center.With the inauguration of the well equipped and modern terminal, the Airport now able to provided better and comfortable environment for its passengers.The operation of the Airport is managed by UPT Ditjen Hubud, an agency under Ministry of Transportation, Indonesia. The airport resides at an elevation of 5 metres (17 ft) above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 03/21 with an asphalt surface measuring 1,170 metres (3,839 ft). There are scheduled commercial flights by Wing Air connecting this Airport to Makassar. Address: Jl.Trans Sulawesi KM 13 Kel.Kasiguncu Kel. Kasiguncu, Kec. Poso Pesisir Kab.
