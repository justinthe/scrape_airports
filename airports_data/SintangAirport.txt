Sintang Airport (IATA: SQG, ICAO: WIOS), also known as Susilo Airport (Indonesian: Bandar Udara Susilo), is an airport serving Sintang, the principal town of the Sintang Regency in West Kalimantan, Indonesia. Terminal Facilities The airport resides at an elevation of 98 ft (30 m) above mean sea level.
