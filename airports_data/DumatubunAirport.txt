Dumatubun Airport is an airport in Langgur, Kai Islands, in the Maluku province of Indonesia. The airport was closed on 19 December 2014 as all flights were moved to the new Karel Sadsuitubun Airport. The Airport now used only by the Indonesian Air Force. The airport resides at an elevation of 3 m (10 ft) above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 09/27 with a concrete surface measuring 1,300 m (6,200 ft) in length.
